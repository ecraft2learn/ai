Logo Menu The Snap ! logo at the left end of the tool bar is clickable. It shows a menu of options about Snap ! itself: The About option displays information about Snap ! itself, including version numbers for the source modules, the implementors, and the license (AGPL: you can do anything with it except create proprietary versions, basically).
