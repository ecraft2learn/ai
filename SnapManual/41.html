41 hexagonal blocks are Predicates, which is the technical term for reporters that report Boolean (true or false) values. Suppose you want to make a block named “square” that draws a square. You would choose Motion, Command, and type “square” into the name ﬁeld. When you click OK, you enter the Block Editor. This works just like making a script in the sprite’s scripting area, except that the “hat” block at the top, instead of saying something like “when I am clicked,” has a picture of the block you’re building. This hat block is called the prototype of your custom block. 1 You drag blocks under the hat to program your custom block, then click OK: Your block appears at the bottom of the Motion palette.
